Knows the rotation.

Pick 1v1, 1v2, or 2v2. Fixed seats, rotating doubles, or winner stays. Chalk owns who breaks next and who partners who.

Ball-by-ball, foul-aware.

Every pot, every miss, every foul gets logged. No more arguments about whether that scratch on the black was a frame-loss.
